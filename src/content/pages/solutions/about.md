# Meet Our Expert Team 

_Bloomwood Solutions delivers expert, friendly IT support in Cairns for homes and small businesses, specialising in solving hi‑tech problems, Wi‑Fi networks, home automation, backups and smart‑tech in general._

 

 

![Steven Bloom, founder of Bloomwood Solutions](/images/solutions/uploads/2025/01/IMG_7304.jpeg) 

 

Bloomwood Solutions founder, Steven Bloom, has loved technology since he was a young boy. With a career spanning enterprise technology he’s been part of deploying hardware and software systems across the Asia Pacific region. 

As the technology guru in the family, he’s always been the goto guy for getting things fixed or working. He’s familiar with all types of home and office technology and getting things working the way they should. 

[Steven’s LinkedIn profile](https://linkedin.com/in/stevenbloom) 

His experience includes: 
 
- Network router installations, maintenance, upgrades 
- Storage Area Networks (SAN) 
- Direct Attached Storage (DAS) 
- Network Attached Storage (NAS) 
- Home automation with specialisation in Apple HomeKit 
- Network printing 
  

 

 

His own home network includes: 
 
- Synology Router connected to Fibre To The Building NBN 
- Synology NAS with 12TB 
- Apple HomeKit for home automation 
- LIFX lighting  
- EVE Home power points 
- 2 x Apple TV 
 
  - 2 x Apple HomePods configured as stereo for an Apple TV 
  - Apple TV and HomePod mini for guests 
  
- 6 x HomePod Minis 
- Eufy Home Security Camera system with 4 solar-powered cameras – integrated into HomeKit 
- iMac 
- Mac Studio with 16TB direct attached RAID 5 storage 
- 2 x iPads 
- 2 x iPhones 
- Ubiquity Unifi WiFi PoE network and switches  
 

He has fixed people systems that were virus ridden, replaced and upgraded PCs and MACs, provided instructions and manuals for scanning, transferring photos, backing up computers, and all the things that people seem to struggle with in todays high tech world. 

In the past he has been known as “The Oracle” because of his breadth of technical experience and ability to solve complex problems. With a recent move to Cairns in Far North Queensland, he’s starting again, providing personalised service to those who need it.  

Whatever your problem, he’ll find a way to help. If he can fix it, he will. If it needs replacing, he will provide recommendations and options.
