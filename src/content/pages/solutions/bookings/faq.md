## Frequently asked questions

### How do I make a booking?
Use the booking button on this site (Cal.com). If you have an urgent request, use the urgent request page.

### Do you offer pre-paid service packs?
Yes — see the pre-paid service packs page for current options and terms.

### What areas do you service?
See the service area page for the current list.

### Can I reschedule or cancel?
Yes. Please use the link in your booking confirmation, or contact us if you’re stuck.
