# Technical Writing


Bloomwood Media is a specialist technical writing practise.  We offer services that help individuals and organisations of all sizes create


- products

- documents

- manuals and

- training curriculum.

- business plans,

- presentations,

- blogs,

- books,

- and assist with the structure and essential contents of statements of work (we’re not lawyers so you’ll still need to get it looked at by someone to ensure it is a legally binding document).

The team consists of seasoned IT professionals that have careers spanning a collective total of four decades and experience in a diverse range of products and technologies, consulting and services.  We have worked with leading global technology vendors and service providers like Medallia, EMC², Compuware, Mercury, HP Software, CA Technologies, Document Sciences, Fuji Xerox Australia, Revolution IT just to name a few.

Some of the customers we have worked with over the years include AMP, Suncorp, Australian Government, Citibank, BUPA, Westpac, ASB Bank NZ, Bank South Pacific, and Genworth Financial.

We pride ourselves in taking complex technical content and turning it into readable material suited for its purpose.
