# Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 10 Nov 2010

This Privacy Policy is intended to inform you about how Steven Bloom Pty Ltd trading as Bloomwood Media (Queensland, Australia) collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your personal information when you visit our website and interact with our services.

## Information we collect

1. **Personal information** – name, email, address, phone number, and any other info you submit via forms or communication channels.
2. **Usage information** – device/browser info and how you interact with the site (e.g., IP address, browser type, access times, referring websites).

## How we use your information

1. **Providing services** – respond to inquiries and deliver requested services.
2. **Communication** – send updates/newsletters/marketing communications (you can unsubscribe).
3. **Analytics** – improve the website/services and user experience.

## Disclosure of your information

We may share your personal information with:

1. **Service providers** (under confidentiality).
2. **Legal obligations** (when required).
3. **Business transfers** (merger/acquisition/asset sale).

## Your choices and rights

You may have rights to access/correct/delete your personal information and withdraw consent (where applicable). Contact us to exercise these rights.

## Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data, but no transmission/storage method is 100% secure.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy over time and will publish changes with an updated effective date.
