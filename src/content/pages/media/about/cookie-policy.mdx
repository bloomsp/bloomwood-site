## What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when a website is loaded in your browser. Cookies help the website function properly, make it more secure, provide better user experience, and help us understand how the website performs so we can improve it.

## How do we use cookies?

As with most online services, our website uses first-party and third-party cookies for several purposes.

- **First-party cookies** are mostly necessary for the website to function the right way and do not collect personally identifiable data.
- **Third-party cookies** are mainly for understanding how the website performs, how you interact with the website, keeping services secure, providing relevant advertisements, and improving user experience.
